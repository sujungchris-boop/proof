The brief

Gensyn — a decentralized machine-learning project — wanted a Korea Blockchain Week mixer that put its founders in the room with Korea's developer and founder community, around decentralized ML, verifiable compute and next-gen AI training.

What we did

At Frame Seoul in Gangnam, PROOF produced the evening end to end — venue, staging and operations — including a live demo of BlockAssist, an AI assistant that learns user behaviour inside Minecraft. Co-founder & CEO Ben Fielding and the team connected with local developers, project founders and industry leaders over open networking.

What we handled
  • Venue build & production
  • Run-of-show & on-site ops
  • Demo staging (BlockAssist)
  • F&B & networking
