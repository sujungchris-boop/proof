The brief

IXO is TokenPost's Web3 trend roadshow and conference series. PROOF was the production partner for all three seasons — from the Season 1 launch in 2023 through Season 3 in 2025 — tasked with growing both scale and polish each year.

What we did

Season 1 (2023, GS Tower Amoris) launched the series to 1,000+ guests. Season 2 (2024, Monaco Space) — 'Ultimate Web3 Roadshow: Embrace the Future' — added booth exhibitions and project showcases. Season 3 (2025, Korea Textile Center) — 'Proof of Business' — went B2C and experiential, with 11+ companies including TRON, Chiliz, Avalanche, OKX Wallet, CertiK, IoTeX and OneKey, plus policy, economics and investment speakers.

The outcome

Across the three seasons PROOF ran main-stage production, exhibition zones, panels and sponsor booths — a cumulative 5,000+ attendees, 100+ sponsors and 50+ sessions, with each edition larger and tighter than the last.

What we handled
  • Main-stage production
  • Booth & exhibition zones
  • Panels & session operations
  • Sponsor booth management
