The brief

A Korean Web3 project needed a sponsor side event during TOKEN2049 in Singapore — a city they had no operating footprint in. They had shortlisted agencies; two of them had never actually run an event in Singapore before.

What we did

Because we already held the venue contacts, AV vendors and staffing relationships, we moved fast. Briefed on a Monday, we had a confirmed venue by Wednesday, then handled vendors, AV and on-site execution through the event. This was a side event during TOKEN2049 — not the conference itself.

The outcome

The client got certainty in an unfamiliar city, on a deadline, during one of the busiest weeks of the Web3 calendar. No improvising on the fly.
